Gearbox Assembly & FEA Analysis

Home | Projects

Project Overview

This project involves the design and analysis of a gearbox assembly. The goal was to optimize the design for efficiency, durability, and manufacturability.

Design Process

The gearbox was designed in SolidWorks, ensuring that the gears, shafts, and casing align perfectly. Materials were selected based on strength and weight considerations.

FEA Analysis

A Finite Element Analysis (FEA) was performed using ANSYS to evaluate the stress distribution under load. The analysis helped in optimizing the gear profile and material selection.

Key Learnings

This project improved my skills in mechanical design, stress analysis, and gear optimization. Future improvements include refining the gear ratios for better performance.
